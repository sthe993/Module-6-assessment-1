ANIMALS MAP PLACES

MONKEY-TRAIL

Monkey Trail is a 10 mile moderately trafficked loop trail located near Keithville, Louisiana that features beautiful wild flowers and is rated as moderate. The trail is primarily used for hiking, running, horses, and mountain biking and is accessible year-round. Dogs are also able to use this trail but must be kept on leash.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

Monkeys can be found all around the map. Since the maps are procedurally generated, you'll have to find them in your own map. Once you've found a monkey, you'll have to cook them a Monkey Meal in a Campfire. Sometimes Monkeys will be trapped in cages. These will join you immediately after you free them.
